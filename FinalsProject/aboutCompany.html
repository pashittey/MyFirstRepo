GADGETRONICS INC.

GADGETRONICS Inc.; commonly known as GADGETRONICS is an American multinational hardware and electronics corporation, specializing in advanced electronics technology, headquartered in Detroit Michigan. GADGETRONICS's products include desktop PCs, laptop PCs (clamshells, 2-in-1s, convertibles and Chromebooks), tablets, servers, storage devices, virtual reality devices, displays, smartphones and peripherals. GADGETRONICS also sells gaming PCs and accessories under its Predator brand.
